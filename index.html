Who We Are

Designed to perform.
Obsessed with craft.

Most businesses don't have a visibility problem. They have a perception problem. The quality is there. The work is real. But the website tells a different story, and customers make their decision in the first three seconds. Kyro was built around one conviction: design isn't decoration, it's leverage. A well-built website doesn't just look good. It earns trust, drives action, and compounds over time.

The businesses that understand this grow faster than the ones that don't. We build for businesses that are serious about growth and serious about craft. Wherever you are, whatever you do, if you're ready to have a website that actually works for you, that's what we do.

How We Work

A process built
to deliver,
every time.

01
Intro Call
Day 1. Free, No Commitment.
Requirements walkthroughScope draftQ&A

We start with a focused call to understand your business, your goals, and exactly what the project needs to accomplish. No pitch, no pressure. Just an honest conversation. From there we draft the scope and requirements together.

02
Contract & Kickoff
Week 1. The Sooner You Sign, The Sooner We Start.
Scope agreedContract signedWork begins

Once the requirements are agreed upon, we send the contract. The clock starts the moment you sign. We don't hold projects in a queue. Sign fast, start fast.

03
First Draft
Weeks 1–2. Built Around Your Brief.
Full designCustom-codedReady to review

Within 1–2 weeks of kickoff, your first draft is ready. Fully designed, fully functional. Not a wireframe or a mood board. A real, working site built around your brief. If you love it, we're done. Most clients do.

04
Revisions & Launch
Weeks 2–4. Finalized Your Way.
Feedback formUp to 2 revision roundsLaunch

If revisions are needed, we handle them through a structured feedback form or a dedicated call, your choice. Up to two revision rounds in the following 1–2 weeks. Projects wrap within 4 weeks from kickoff. Every time. Larger scopes get a custom timeline in the contract upfront.
